About Experience Contacts Projects

I'm Elprofessor, a versatile African who wears many hats - Market Researcher, Concept Developer, Copywriter, and Solution Specialist. My work revolves around understanding people, data, and situations, using skills like Communication, Improvisation, Commerce Development, Research and Analysis, Laterality, and Micro-solutions Hacking.

What makes me stand out is my deep understanding of blockchain technology, where I not only navigate its complexities but also work as an independent consultant for Bitcoin brands.

Beyond processing information, my goal is to produce results that bring a fresh perspective, especially in the blockchain space. I specialize in analyzing information to help businesses reshape and thrive.

Alongside my growing copywriting business, I actively collaborate with organizations and teams identified during my blockchain research. As an independent consultant for Bitcoin brands, I reach out when I see opportunities to provide valuable insights. My aim is to build relationships that benefit both parties, aligning my unique skills with the specific needs of Bitcoin-oriented ventures.

Passionate about making a positive impact, especially in the dynamic world of Bitcoin, I embrace new challenges that push conventional thinking. I invite you to connect and explore how my diverse skill set and experience in Bitcoin consultancy can benefit your brand and endeavors.

Embarking on my ghostwriting journey in 2017, I've contributed to the narratives of over 20 diverse brands. Notable among them are ByteSized Bitcoin, E5 Club, CampGaiad, Golems of Fortune, Career Bears NFT, Secret Space Virtual Vault, and Rift Xtended Reality Protocol.

From demystifying cryptocurrency for ByteSized Bitcoin to bridging fantasy and finance with Golems of Fortune, each collaboration has been a unique exploration. Whether elevating the exclusivity of elite networking or unlocking the mysteries of virtual and augmented reality, my role as a ghostwriter has shaped innovative and captivating stories for these brands, connecting them with audiences on a profound level. As I reflect on these experiences, I eagerly anticipate the unwritten tales that lie ahead in my journey.
